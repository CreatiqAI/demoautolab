## What we collect

**When you create an account**, we collect your name, phone number, email address and password. You may also add a username, date of birth, gender and delivery address.

**When you tell us about your vehicle**, we store the car make and model you select, so we can show you parts that fit.

**When you place an order**, we collect your delivery address, contact number, the items ordered, the payment method chosen, and the order's payment and delivery status. Card and online banking payments are processed by our payment provider — we receive confirmation that a payment succeeded or failed, and we do not store your full card number.

**As you trade with us**, we build a commercial record: your order history, lifetime and monthly spending, membership tier, reward points, vouchers held, and any credit limit we extend to you.

**If you register as a merchant or vendor**, we collect your business details and the bank or payout details needed to pay you.

**If you list an item on the second-hand marketplace or write a review**, we store what you publish, along with your identity as its author.

**When you contact us**, including over WhatsApp, we keep the conversation and the phone number it came from.

**When you use the site**, we collect technical data through cookies and similar technologies: your device, IP address, and how you move around the site.

## Why we use it, and on what basis

- **To perform our contract with you** — creating your account, processing and delivering orders, taking payment, handling returns and refunds, and providing support. Without this data we cannot supply you.
- **Because we have a legitimate interest** — securing the platform against fraud and abuse, understanding how the site is used so we can improve it, and calculating the tiers, points and pricing you qualify for.
- **Because the law requires it** — keeping tax, accounting and transaction records for the periods Malaysian law prescribes.
- **With your consent** — sending you marketing about products and promotions. You can withdraw this at any time, and doing so will not affect the service you receive.

## Marketing, and how to stop it

We send marketing by email and WhatsApp only where you have agreed to receive it. Every marketing message includes a way to opt out, and you can change your preferences at any time in your notification settings, or by replying to tell us to stop.

Withdrawing consent to marketing does not stop transactional messages — order confirmations, delivery updates and return outcomes — because those are part of supplying you.

## Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing.

We share it only where necessary:

- **Couriers**, so they can deliver your order. They receive your name, address and phone number.
- **Payment providers**, to take and reconcile payment.
- **Vendors on the platform**, where you buy an item they fulfil. They receive what is needed to fulfil that order.
- **Our service providers** — hosting, messaging and IT support — who process data on our instructions under contract and may not use it for anything else.
- **Authorities**, where the law, a court, or an enforceable government request obliges us.

## Where your data is held

Our platform and its data are hosted on infrastructure that may be located outside Malaysia. Where personal data leaves Malaysia we take reasonable steps to ensure it remains protected to a standard comparable to the PDPA, and we transfer it only to providers bound by a written agreement to protect it.

## How long we keep it

- **Your account** — while it is open, and for a reasonable period afterwards.
- **Orders, invoices and payment records** — for at least seven years, as tax and accounting law requires.
- **Marketing preferences** — until you withdraw consent, plus a record of that withdrawal so we can honour it.
- **Support conversations** — for as long as needed to resolve the matter and handle any dispute arising from it.

When data is no longer needed for the purpose it was collected for, and we are not required to keep it, we delete it or render it anonymous.

## Your rights

Under the PDPA you may:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you, and ask for a copy.
- **Correct** it, if it is inaccurate, incomplete or out of date.
- **Withdraw consent** to processing that relies on consent, including marketing.
- **Limit** how we process your data, including asking us not to use it for direct marketing.
- **Complain** to the Personal Data Protection Commissioner if you believe we have mishandled your data.

To exercise any of these, contact us at [DPO EMAIL]. We may ask you to verify your identity first. We will respond within the period the PDPA allows, and we will tell you if we cannot do what you have asked and why. We may charge a small prescribed fee for a data access request.

## Keeping it secure

We protect personal data with technical and organisational measures appropriate to the risk, including encryption in transit, access controls, and restricting access to staff who need it to do their job.

No system is perfectly secure. If a breach occurs that is likely to cause you significant harm, we will notify you and the Commissioner as the PDPA requires.

## Cookies

We use cookies to keep you signed in, remember what is in your cart, and understand how the site is used so we can improve it. You can refuse cookies in your browser settings, but parts of the site — including signing in and checking out — will not work properly without them.

## Children

The platform is intended for businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has given us their data, contact us and we will delete it.

## Other websites

This policy covers 12V only. If you follow a link to another site, that site's own privacy practices apply, and we are not responsible for them. Read their policy before giving them your data.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. If a change materially affects your rights or how we use your data, we will give you notice by a prominent notice on the site or a message to you. Continuing to use the platform after a change takes effect means you accept the updated policy.
